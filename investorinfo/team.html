About the Team

Derek Evans — Founder & Builder

Navy-trained airframer turned entrepreneur. Co-founded Granite Ledge Coffee with my father and spent years building systems that keep small teams effective. Heirloom is my long-term mission: a private, durable memory OS for families that replaces platform lock-in with ownership and longevity.

I’m obsessed with clear, durable design: client-side encryption, local export, and human-readable stories that your grandkids can still open. The plan is SaaS first for speed, then hardware for the families who want total local control.
