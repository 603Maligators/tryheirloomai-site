Problem

Families lose voices, videos, and stories as years pass. Personal histories end up scattered across devices and platforms, and existing solutions are fragmented and rarely privacy-focused. The result is a slow erosion of the people and moments that shape us.

Solution

Heirloom is a journaling and memory-capture platform that lets families record text, voice, and video in one secure place. Our AI recalls and weaves these memories into rich narratives while preserving full privacy, giving families an effortless way to save and relive their lives.

Product
